Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how data is handled by VinylX, a private, internal automation tool built and operated solely by George, operating as VinylX. VinylX is used exclusively by its owner to automate posting content to the owner's own TikTok channel. It is not a commercial product and has no end users.

2. Data Collection

VinylX does not collect any personal data from third parties. No user accounts exist, no forms are submitted, and no information is gathered from visitors or external individuals. The only data handled by VinylX is:

  • The owner's TikTok OAuth access token, used to authenticate with the TikTok Developer API.

3. Data Storage

The TikTok OAuth access token is stored locally on the owner's own machine. No data is stored on external servers, cloud platforms, or third-party databases.

4. Cookies, Analytics, and Tracking

VinylX uses no cookies, no analytics services, and no tracking mechanisms of any kind. This website is a static informational page and does not employ any client-side tracking.

5. Data Sharing

No data is sold, rented, traded, or shared with any third party. The owner's authentication credentials are used solely to interact with the TikTok API on the owner's behalf.

6. Third-Party Services

VinylX interacts with TikTok via the TikTok Developer API. TikTok's handling of data on their platform is governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy. VinylX does not control or influence how TikTok processes data.

7. GDPR Compliance

VinylX does not collect or process personal data of any EU residents or any other individuals. Since no third-party personal data is handled, GDPR data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure, etc.) are not applicable in the context of this tool.

8. Children's Privacy

VinylX does not collect data from any individuals, including children. The tool has no public-facing interface and no mechanism for any person to submit information.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at the owner's discretion. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
